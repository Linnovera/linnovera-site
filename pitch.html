The Problem

Consumers who care about natural fibres have no reliable way to search by composition. Retailer product pages bury fibre data in inconsistent formats. "Organic cotton blend" might mean 90% cotton or 40%. There is no standardized, searchable, verified source for what's actually in your clothes.

Greenwashing thrives in this gap — and consumers have no tool to verify claims after purchase.

The Solution

LinnoVera is a fibre composition verification engine. We normalize heterogeneous retailer data into a unified, searchable taxonomy of 72+ fibre types. Every product is verified against the retailer's own stated composition with a timestamp and URL.

Premium features include AI-powered deep search with confidence scoring, shareable Composition Cards, longitudinal Wardrobe Scoring, and greenwashing change-detection alerts.

Differentiation

LinnoVera is not a creator-driven affiliate platform like LTK or ShopMy. It is a verification-first search engine. Products are ranked by composition quality, not by influencer endorsement or paid placement.

The company is positioned at the intersection of three growing markets: sustainable fashion ($8.25B → $33B by 2030), AI-powered shopping, and consumer demand for supply chain transparency.

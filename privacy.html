Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026 · Last Updated March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

LinnoVera, Inc. (“LinnoVera,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website linnovera.com and the LinnoVera application (collectively, the “Platform”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Platform.

LinnoVera is a Canadian federal corporation headquartered in Ontario, Canada. We comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL), and applicable provincial privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you create a profile, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Profile preferences: Fibre preferences, size preferences, budget ranges, and shopping-for designations stored in your LinnoVera profiles.
  • Saved items: Products you save, searches you bookmark, and wardrobe items you log.
  • Newsletter subscription: Your email address when you subscribe to the LinnoVera newsletter.
  • Feedback: Information you provide when reporting broken URLs, suggesting retailers, or contacting us.
  • Wardrobe data: If you use our wardrobe feature, the fibre composition data and photos you voluntarily enter about your existing garments.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, filters applied, search queries entered, products viewed, and links clicked. This data is collected in aggregate to improve the Platform and is not sold to third parties.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and device type.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to maintain your session and save your profile preferences. See Section 7 below.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment information. All purchases happen on retailer websites, not on LinnoVera.
  • We do not collect government identification numbers.
  • We do not track your browsing activity on other websites.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Platform: Displaying search results, maintaining your profiles and saved items, calculating your wardrobe composition score, and delivering personalized product recommendations.
  • To send the newsletter: If you subscribe, we send a weekly email with curated fibre content. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To improve the Platform: Analysing aggregate usage patterns to understand which features are used and how we can improve.
  • To respond to inquiries: Addressing feedback, broken URL reports, and support requests.
  • To generate anonymized trend data: We may compile anonymized, aggregated data about fibre search trends and category popularity. This data contains no personal information.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We never have and never will.

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: If you explicitly choose to share (e.g., sharing a Composition Card on social media).
  • Service providers: Trusted third-party services that operate the Platform (hosting, email delivery, analytics), contractually required to protect your information.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice provided to you.
  • Anonymized data: We may share anonymized, aggregated trend data that cannot identify any individual.

5. Affiliate Links and Retailer Interactions

LinnoVera earns affiliate commissions when you click through our product links and make a purchase on a retailer’s website. When you click an affiliate link, you leave LinnoVera and enter the retailer’s website. The retailer (not LinnoVera) collects your purchase information. The affiliate network may set a cookie on your device to track the referral. LinnoVera receives notification of the referral and commission amount, but we do not receive your payment details, order details, or personal information from the retailer.

Our editorial selections and product verification are independent of affiliate relationships. No brand pays to appear in our database.

6. Data Retention

  • Profile data: Retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time.
  • Newsletter data: Retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Usage data: Retained in anonymized form for up to 24 months.
  • Feedback submissions: Retained for the period necessary to address the issue.

7. Cookies

LinnoVera uses cookies for the following purposes only:

  • Essential cookies: To maintain your session, remember your profile selection, and save your search preferences. Necessary for the Platform to function.
  • Analytics cookies: To understand aggregate usage patterns. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit your ability to use profile and saved search features.

8. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information and account.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • Complaint: File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

9. Children’s Privacy

LinnoVera does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our children’s clothing categories are intended for use by parents and guardians. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Users

LinnoVera is operated from Canada. If you access the Platform from outside Canada, your information may be transferred to and processed in Canada. By using the Platform, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Material changes will be notified by email or prominent notice on the Platform.
